Recipe of Biryani

Ingredients for chicken marination

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken (bone- in preferred) 500g
Yogurt 1 cup
Ginger-garlic paste 1tbsp
Turmeric powder 1tbsp
Red chilli powder 1tbsp
Garam masala 1tbsp
Salt Adjust to taste
Lemon juice 1tbsp

For the rice

Ingredients Quantity
Basmati rice 2cups
Water 4 cups
Cloves 2-3
Cardamom pods 2
Bay leaf 1
Salt Adjust to taste


For layering

Ingredients Quantity
Saffron (optional) or little yellow food color 2 tbsp
Ghee or Butter 4 tbsp
Fried onions

    Step 1: Cook the rice

  • Boil 5 cups water with whole spices and salt.
  • Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done (grains should be firm).
  • Drain and keep aside.

    Step 2: Prepare chicken masala

  • Heat oil/ghee in a deep pan. Add whole spices.
  • Add onions and fry until golden brown.
  • Stir in ginger-garlic paste, sauté until fragrant.
  • Add tomatoes, chili, turmeric, and garam masala. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • Mix in yogurt, then add chicken pieces. Cook until chicken is tender and masala thick.
  • Add coriander and mint leaves.

    Step 3: Layering

  • In a heavy-bottomed pot, spread half the chicken masala.
  • Layer half the rice on top.
  • Sprinkle saffron milk, fried onions, a drizzle of ghee.
  • Repeat with remaining chicken and rice layers.
  • Cover with a tight lid (or seal with dough) and cook on very low heat ("dum") for 20–25 minutes.

    Step 4: Serve

  • Gently mix before serving.
  • Garnish with fried onions, mint, and serve hot with raita or salad.
